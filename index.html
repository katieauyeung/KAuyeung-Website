Hello, my name is Katie Auyeung and I am a second year English major with junior class standing at the University of California, Riverside.

I currently work as a campus tour guide under UCR Undergraduate Admissions. As a tour guide, I serve as a campus ambassador to guests and prospective students who visit our university. The primary objective of my position is to present a comprehensive and thorough overview of buildings, student life, and academic activities to those who are interested in attending UCR by highlighting the many resources and opportunities that our campus offers. I am trained to provide both group and family tours and am experienced in providing various levels of information to a wide variety of audiences, including high school groups, incoming first year students, and transfer students.

I have also been a member of the Archery Club at UCR since October 2021, and am serving as the current president of the club as of June 2022. As president of the Archery Club at UCR, I work with my officer board and our advisor and coach to ensure that our club and its activities are consistently aligned with our mission to introduce archery to a wider population at UC Riverside. I serve as the primary point of contact between our student organization and UCR Student Life as well as the competitive sports department, and communicate our responsibilities as an organization on campus to my officers in order to ensure that we keep our club in good standing. I also work to coordinate and organize practice times, competitions and tournaments, and off-practice club activities in order to create an environment where our members can grow as individuals, athletes, and archers alongside each other.
